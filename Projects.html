Human Motion Project, 2016 - Present

From starting a project wanting to build a prosthetic foot to designing a portable human motion measurement system today, this project has changed a lot over the years. The Human Motion Project seeks to expand accessibility of human motion data to remote and underpriveledged places by creating a system that is based on inertial sensors instead of camera arrays. The other members of this project team and I are currently seeking a patent and sponsorship since this project began to show promise.

Minibeacon Project, 2018 - Present

In the weather balloon research group HARBOR, we track the balloon in multiple ways. The Minibeacon is a line-of-sight tracker mainly used as tertiary tracking and for recovery. One device flies with the balloon, transmitting its position data to a ground unit that indicates the distance, azimuth, and angle of elevation to the balloon.
