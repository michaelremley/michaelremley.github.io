Spring 2018

Summary
We built a walking robot, called the Wanderer, that uses OpenCV to see people as it wanders around. When it sees people, it greets them, and when the people leave it says goodbye. I learned about robotic systems integration in this project by designing power circuitry and connecting all electrical hardware for the software subteam. I also worked in the mechanical subteam to ensure internal components had enough space in the final assembly. Project Website
